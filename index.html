Why Buffalo Drinks?

We are on a mission to offer a healthy and sustainable choice in energy drinks, with no compromise on taste, quality or most important of all – experience.

LOW CALORIES

Buffalo Energy is not only refreshing in taste, it boosts mood and abilities without excessive calories.

OWN YOUR MOMENT

Let no moment pass by without knowing you have made the most of it with Buffalo Energy.

TAURINE FREE

Being a vegan product, there is absolutely no taurine in our drinks. Enjoy every sip of Buffalo Energy in the comfort of knowing there’s no animal by-products being consumed.

VEGAN PRODUCT

We are different in many ways, and our commitment to only use plant-based ingredients ensures that Buffalo Energy not only stands out from the others, it caters to almost every individual.
